About WordRocket AI

WordRocket AI was founded in 2019 by a team of AI engineers and business strategists who recognized a critical gap in the market. While AI technology was becoming increasingly powerful, most businesses didn't have access to practical, affordable solutions to implement it. We started with a simple mission: democratize AI automation and make it accessible to businesses of all sizes.
